Hi, 

I just committed a modification of UserInputPanel, which allows to
specify fields based on conditions.

e.g.
<panel id="environment">  
	<field type="title" id="environment.settings.title" size="1.5"
bold="true" icon="tools"/>
	<field type="staticText" align="left"
id="environment.settings.long.description.text"
conditionid="windowstarget" /> 
      	<field type="staticText" align="left"
id="environment.settings.unix.long.description.text"
conditionid="unixtarget" />
</panel>

which creates a panel, that shows information dependent on the
conditions with the ids windowstarget and unixtarget. This conditions
have to be specified in conditions.xml
