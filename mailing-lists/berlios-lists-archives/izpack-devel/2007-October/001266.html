Hello everybody,

For the needs of my company, I've developed an (nice ;-) installer thanks to izpack. While Izpack is really 
great, I had special summary requirements. This is why I imagined an automatic summary feature  for 
UserInputPanel (UIP in short) and for ConditionalUIP.

So, I implemented getSummaryBody() and getSummaryCaption() in subclasses of UIP and ConditionalUIP that
scans the xml description of the UIP and gathers the value of each field that directly has a variable. (so we have
no summary for password fields, option fields, etc. in the SummaryPanel; it's enough for my current needs).

For that purpose I had to modify the UserInputPanel.java in order to set 'langpack' and 'spec' in protected (rather
than in private). I think (should I say hope ?) that doesn't break the design.

My question is simple: is there a prefered way/person to send my new java classes + eventual screenshots ?
May I do that in this mailing list?

Best regards and thanks again for this excellent software,
