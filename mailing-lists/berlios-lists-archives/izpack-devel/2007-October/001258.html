I had a problem with the ShortcutPanel.

The ShortcutPanel was displayed after a summary panel and before a datacheck panel (that last for debug purpose).
The first time the ShortcutPanel is displayed, everything is ok and works fine.

That means that once used, it was not possible to use it again...
