Hi all,

IzPack 3.9.0 is edging closer to the final release, which could  
happen as soon as the end of this month. We need your help!

Starting from now, no more features will be added to 3.9, and a  
branch has been created. The goal is now to focus on spotting  
potential problems and fix them for the final release. This release  
is the first of two milestone releases before entering the release  
candidates stages. The next milestone will focus on the documentation.

You can get IzPack 3.9.0-M1 from ftp://ftp.berlios.de/pub/izpack/ 
IzPack-install-3.9.0-M1.jar, and the corresponding source code from  
our Subversion repository:
* http://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/izpack/izpack-src/branches/ 
branch-3.9/
* http://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/izpack/izpack-src/tags/3.9.0-M1/

The IzPack roadmap is managed through Google Calendar, and you can  
follow it through various formats, see http://www.izforge.com/izpack/ 
developers for more details.

Thanks in advance for your feedback!

Cheers
