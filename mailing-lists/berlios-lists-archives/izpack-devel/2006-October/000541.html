Hi,
with 3.9.0 the layout has changed from sometimes this
sometimes something other to IzPanelLayout.
Now you can configure the layout in your install.xml.
E.g. you can change the anchor of all panels with one
entry.
For an example see the <guiprefs> section
%IZ_HOME%\src\dist-files\IzPack-install.xml
The docu contains a discription in 
Advanced FeaturesAdvanced Features->Modifying IzPack Panels

Cheers

Klaus
