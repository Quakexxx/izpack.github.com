Hi everyone,

I have just checked-in to the trunk the following updates.

1. The Liquid and JGoodies look and feels have been updated to their
latest versions.

2. The Substance look and feel is now supported (see
http://svn.berlios.de/viewcvs/izpack?rev=1836&view=rev). The supported
themes are from the toned-down family
(https://substance.dev.java.net/docs/skins/toneddown.html) as the
other ones are either too dark or too saturated :-)

Cheers
