Klaus pointed out a good point: We start our installer out of a native
exe (we use an NSIS wrapper for this in order to have all data inside
the executable, but an other one would do the trick too).

As far as I can see, microsoft recommends the way of using a "manifest",
but I could not find a docu for this.

Of corse, you have the possibility of lauching the installer explicitely
as a (elevated) Superuser, but you have to think about it before
doubleclicking the installer's jar...

Look at the bugreports at sun's side, and then you know that you will
not get any support for this issue from the makers of java...
