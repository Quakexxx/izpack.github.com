Hi Julien,

I am not currently active in the project so my opinion does not way all that
much.

I think it is good that you think about potentially using another version
control system, since this might make life easier for a lot of folks.
Probably this should primarily be a function of what most folks routinely
use for other purposes, not necessarily of the strength or support for other
applications. In the end it tends to be the easiest if people do not have to
work with too many different ways of performing what is essentially the same
task.

In a way this might be a good opportunity for performing a little unofficial
poll on what version control systems are actually in wide spread use.

	Elmar
