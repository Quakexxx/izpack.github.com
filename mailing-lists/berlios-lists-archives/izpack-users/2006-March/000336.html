installed via izpack. So I use the attribute initialState="noShow" in the 
shortcut config. But it does not work. What I have made wrong?

<shortcut
     name="104You"
     programGroup="yes"
     desktop="yes"
     applications="yes"
     startMenu="yes"
     startup="no"
