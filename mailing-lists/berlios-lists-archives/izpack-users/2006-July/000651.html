Not sure to understand your real need.
But if you intend to keep some files (archives or whatever...) after
uninstall, we don't have this option available at the moment.
The thing you could do as a work around would be to use the process panel to
copy the archives whereever you want and then when uninstalling DO NOT check
the force deletion.
This way files added would be left. (At least i think so... give it a try.)
