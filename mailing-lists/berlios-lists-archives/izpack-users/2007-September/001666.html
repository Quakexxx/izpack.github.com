You'll have to define a condition on that and define your fields twice like this:

 

  <field type="search" variable="KS_FILE" conditionid="filevarset">

      <description type="file" />

      <spec result="file" id="input2.searchdes" size="30">

          <choice txt="${KS_FILE}" />

      </spec>

  </field>

  

  <field type="search" variable="KS_FILE" conditionid="!filevarset">

      <description type="file" />

      <spec result="file" id="input2.searchdes" size="30">

          <choice txt="" />

      </spec>

  </field>

 

filevarset has to be defined in your conditions.xml and you'll have to implement a Condition (subclass com.izforge.izpack.rules.Condition) which check's if a variable is null or not.
