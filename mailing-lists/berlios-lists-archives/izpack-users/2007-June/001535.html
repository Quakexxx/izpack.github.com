Hi Thomas,
until now there is no option to disable freespace check.
You can comment out it in your local version of IzPack.
com.izforge.izpack.panels.PacksPanelBase line 236 and 256.
Or expand 
com.izforge.izpack.util.IoHelper.extractLong

The problem will be the thousands separator in your output.

I wonder about your output of dir /-C. On my box (XP)
dir /? brings the info that /-C suppress the separators.
Do you have an own dir command?

Any body else seen this behavior?
