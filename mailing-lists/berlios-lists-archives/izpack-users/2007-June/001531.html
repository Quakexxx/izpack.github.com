Hi Thomas,
free disk space will be reported from the disk where $INSTALL_PATH
will be created. If you do not use the TargetPanel or the TargetPanel
is behind PacksPanel a default will be used for the disk. My be this
disk is full.
If not. Open a cmd.exe and write
dir /D /-C <disk>
What last line is written?
