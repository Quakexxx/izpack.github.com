not that you mean nobody reads your mail...
But I do not really know why it should not work. Therefore I have waited

I use the feature since some years.
 
Or do you have NOT added shortcutSpec.xml as resource? E.g.
  <res id="shortcutSpec.xml"
src="product/appl/install/IzPackProjects/AdminClients/ShortcutSpec.xml"/
>
Look into your install.jar. Are there 
native/ShellLink.dll
res/shortcutSpec.xml
 
Look for the spelling, the res id beginns with a short "s". If you have
used a "S", the Shortcut panel will not
find the spec.
