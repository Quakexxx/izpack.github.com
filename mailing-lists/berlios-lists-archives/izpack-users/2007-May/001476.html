Hi John,
if you declare no  <guiprefs> for your installation 
(see docu paragraph "The GUI Preferences Element <guiprefs>")
you installatin uses "native" LAF. On some OSes the VM means that
"metal" is the native LAF.
You can declare other LAFs for all or a single OS. See also docu.
