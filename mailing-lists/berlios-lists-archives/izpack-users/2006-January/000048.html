Hi,

dunno if anybody replied to you regarding 1.5 ?
Anyway, just in case. please avoid using 1.5 at the moment. You would rather
use 1.4.2_xx since it's the only fully working version of java so far. 1.5
is still under development and very buggy. We had lots of issues related to
it. IzPack doesn't support yet 1.5 fully.
To avoid you some troubles, i would advise you to switch to 1.4.2, at least
for IzPack.
