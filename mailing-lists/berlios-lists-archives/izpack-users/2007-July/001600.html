Ok, so I was being stupid.
Answering my own question:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="iso-8859-1" standalone="yes" ?>
<langpack>
    <str id="core" txt="My Product"/>
    <str id="core.description">
This is line 1 of the description.
This is line 2.
     </str>
</langpack>
